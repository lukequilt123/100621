The Untapped process

Six stages. One very different way to hire.

Every stage is built to reveal the real person, not the rehearsed one.

STAGE 01

Advertising

Distinctive, visually attractive adverts that get great response rates and draw the right people in.

STAGE 02

CVs read last

You don't read a single CV until you've met and spent time with the candidates. People first, paper second.

STAGE 03

Selection Days

Days designed to test culture fit, behaviour and attitude as well as skill set, not a list of pre-prepared questions.

STAGE 04

Two-way process

Candidates decide if your business is right for them too, through a live Q&A with you on the day.

STAGE 05

Retention

Fit is tested at the outset for both sides, so our candidates stay in post longer.

STAGE 06

Coaching

Independent coaching support for your new hire for three months, so they integrate quickly and effectively.
